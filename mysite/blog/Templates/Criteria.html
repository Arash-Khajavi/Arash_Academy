The applicants who apply must :

  • have already completed the year they wish to work in
  • have achieved high grades in their field of expertise and related subjects
  • pass the entrance test
  • get accepted by our accredited psychologist

The procedure

  1. Our future peers have to initially have sign up on the peer's sign up page where the report cards of the last two academic years should be uploaded.
  2. Following our coordinator's approval, the shortlisted candidates will be invited to an entrance exam where they will be assessed on their academic skills.
  3. In case of passing the minimum requirements, the accepted candidates will be invited to an interview with our accredited psychologist.

The prices for each peer will be estimated by our board of directors and discussed with the peer.
For younger tutors, volunteering is highly recommended for future university applications.
